How to Play

  1. Choose your starting vocabulary level using the dropdown.
  2. Click "Start Game" to begin.
  3. A Korean word will appear in the center.
  4. Below it, several emojis will be displayed. Click on the emoji that best represents the meaning of the Korean word.
  5. Get 10 correct answers in a row to advance to the next level.
  6. If you choose incorrectly, your "Correct in Level" counter resets, but your total score remains.
  7. The game continues as long as you keep matching words!
  8. Click "Play Again" after the game ends to start over.
